Core Vegan Menu Recipes

Jackfruit Tacos

Slow-cooked jackfruit in smoky chipotle with cabbage slaw and avocado crema.

Yield: ~8 tacos

Ingredients

  • 2 cups young green jackfruit (canned, drained)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp chipotle chili powder
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil and bloom spices with jackfruit.
  2. Cook until tender; shred with fork.
  3. Add tomato sauce + maple; simmer 10 minutes.

Chickpea Curry Bowl

Coconut curry with turmeric rice and roasted veggies.

Yield: 2 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 can chickpeas, drained
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp curry powder, 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 cup roasted vegetables
  • 1 cup cooked rice

Instructions

  1. Sauté onion until golden; add spices.
  2. Simmer with chickpeas + coconut milk 15 min.
  3. Serve over rice with vegetables.

BBQ Tempeh Sandwich

Smoky tempeh with pickled onions, slaw, and house BBQ sauce.

Yield: 2 sandwiches

Ingredients

  • 1 block tempeh, sliced
  • 1/2 cup BBQ sauce
  • Pickled onions, slaw, buns

Instructions

  1. Marinate tempeh in BBQ 20 min; grill or pan-fry.

Southwest Grain Bowl

Quinoa, black beans, corn, roasted sweet potato, kale, lime tahini.

Yield: 2 servings

Instructions

  1. Combine all components; dress with lime tahini and toss.

Spicy Peanut Noodle Salad

Cold soba noodles with peanut-ginger dressing.

Yield: 2 servings

Instructions

  1. Whisk peanut butter, soy sauce, vinegar, ginger.
  2. Toss noodles + veggies with dressing.
